# StageScape Research Sources

The archive was created through publicly available sources.

## Primary Sources

- Official concert videos
- Official concert trailers
- Concert DVD and Blu-ray clips
- Official artist YouTube channels
- Official performance videos

## Secondary Sources

- Concert reviews
- Production-related articles
- Venue information websites
- Entertainment news articles

## Reference Platforms

- YouTube
- HYBE
- SM Entertainment
- YG Entertainment
- JYP Entertainment
- CJ ENM
- Coachella
- Lollapalooza
- KCON

## Research Method

Each concert was analyzed from a production perspective.

The archive focused on:

- Stage technology
- Venue scale
- Visual direction
- Production systems

rather than fan-oriented content.
